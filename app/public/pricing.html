Pricing

Overview

Authorly is free to use for building your storybook app, there are no sign up or usage costs.

  • Sign up, create your apps and publish to the app stores for free


We make money via a revenue share on sales. Once your app is published:

  • The marketplaces (Apple, Google, etc.) collect 30% of your app's total revenue
  • Authorly takes 20% of your app's total revenue
  • You keep the remaining 50% of your app's revenue


If you want us to make the app for you, you can utilize our production service for a small fee.
