Pricing

We Build Your App

Want our experts to build an awesome app for you? When we build your app, we add engaging touch-triggered animations, word-by-word highlighting, narration, and more.



Do-It-Yourself Tool

Want to build your app yourself? No problem. Authorly is free to use for building your storybook app. Once you're ready to publish your app to sell, there is a distribution fee of $250.00 USD for us to publish your application to Apple's iTunes, Google Play and the Amazon Appstore.



How Do I Get Paid?

You can choose to publish your app via your own Apple, Google and Amazon accounts or publish and distribute your app through ours.


If you choose to publish the app through your accounts, you will receive payments directly from Apple, Amazon and Google for your sales.


If you distirbute your app through our accounts, here are a few important pieces of information:

  • Authorly keeps 20% of all sales.
  • Payments are sent quarterly

Whether you choose to distribute your app with us, or use your own accounts, the app stores (Apple, Amazon, Google) will keep 30% of sales generated on their websites.
