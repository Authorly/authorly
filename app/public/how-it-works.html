Overview

Authorly is an online do-it-yourself tool, meaning you can log‐in and use it on any browser.

Anyone with an internet connection and modern browser can build storybook apps. Still sounds difficult? Authorly also offers production services for a small fee to those who want Authorly to build an App for them.
