Features

Read our guide!

Got pictures?

Whether you're a children's author, a comic book artist, a visual novelist, a zine publisher, the curator of the next Codex Seraphinianus, or you just have a great illustrated story to tell, you'll have a blast transforming your masterpiece into an interactive app.

One app fits all platforms

Spend your energy creating, not pushing pixels. Authorly takes care of the tedious aspects of app production, like adjusting your book to fit all the 1000+ smartphones and tablets in the world and exporting it to Google Play, the App Store, and the Kindle Store. You'll have an app that looks splendid on every device–and sells in every venue.

The little app that could

Make your message fit the medium. With Authorly's web-based app engine, you can pull off a number of cool multimedia tricks right from your browser. Need ideas? Start with these:

Embed a video

Add a soundtrack

Animate your characters’ movements

Pan and zoom through your story

Create a narration track

Upload your text and voice files, and Authorly will automatically align them and provide word-by-word highlighting.
