How it works

Read our guide!

1. Import your story's media

Upload your images, videos, sound effects, and custom typefaces right through the web interface. Pull them out of Authorly's media libraries when you need them for a specific page.

2. Drag and drop your design

Authorly keeps track of your pages in the left sidebar, similar to PowerPoint slides or Adobe Acrobat/Reader PDF pages. Create static pages with text and images, or style them up with panning, zooming, hotspots, and background sounds.

3. Add audio storytelling

Upload the audio narration file. Select "auto-align" to have Authorly automatically match the narration to the text of your story, or match the words yourself with the "manually align" option.

4. Preview and submit

Click the Preview button to experience your work through a reader's eyes. Like what you see? It's time to export your storybook as an Android/Apple/Kindle app! (Note: you'll need to sign up for an Authorly plan before exporting.)

Need help on any of these steps? Check out our user manual for detailed how-tos on every Authorly feature, or drop us a line at support@authorly.com.
